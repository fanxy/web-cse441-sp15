Serendipity

Explore the world around you,
online or offline.

What We Do


Problem: Backpackers have difficulty exploring their
surroundings without the use of their cell and data connection.

Solution: Serendipity enables backpackers to explore
and navigate urban attractions online or offline.

Plan

Add places to your journey and plan your trip on the map.

Journey

Navigate to your chosen destinations and change your journey along the way,
online or offline.

Share

Read notes left by other travelers while exploring the city and leave notes for others to discover.
